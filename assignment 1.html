Sea life

Dolphins

Dolphins are one of the many beautiful animals that live in our oceans. They are beautiul elongated mammals that come from the family of whales.

Some important facts about dolphins:

  • Dolphins are carnivores which means they only eat meat
  • They are known for their playful behaviour and are suprising to most people, quite intellectual
  • Many people enjoy going to swim with dolphins while on vacation in places like Hawaii.

Turtles

Sea turtles

Sea turtles have quite a lot of phsyical differences than land turtles. Their shells are light, flat and have a certain pattern which helps them to swim/glide through the water. THey are also omnivores.

Land turtles

Land turtles are also kown as turtoises. They have massive shells and belong to a group of reptiles. Tortoises are also mostly vegetarians. The following is the taxonomic classification of turtoises:

  1. Domain: Eukarya
  2. Kingdom: Animalia
  3. Phylum: Chordata
  4. Class: Reptilia
  5. Order: Testudines
  6. Family: Testudinidae
  7. Type species: Testudo graeca
